Pizza

My decision to make this dish was easy. The first time I made it, all I could remember was the taste of the garlic and cheese from the crust topped with pepperoni.

You will not regret making this for your family. This is a recipe you family will love and beg you to make every night.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bacon
  • Olives

Step by Step

  1. Pre Heat Oven to 425
  2. Drizzle cheese onto crust
  3. Add Meat
  4. Add Veggies
  5. Pop in Oven
  6. Cook for 25 mins
  7. Take out and Eat
