Lasagna

Description

This mouth watering lasagna is a dinner favortie and will not disappoint the family.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bell Peppers
  • Thyme
  • Fire Roasted Tomotates
  • Sundryed Tomotates

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Mix Tomotates & bell peppers in a suace pan and add Thyme
  3. Add fire roasted Tomotates
  4. Add sun dryed Tomotates
  5. Add oven bake lasagna noodles
  6. Bake for 15 mins
  7. Take out of oven and enjoy
