Cheesy Mouth Watering Lasagna

Upon deciding to make this meal, I contemplated 2 other pasta dishes. But soon I realized the cheesy mouthwatering flavor of this lasagna that I cooked for my sister's wedding. I received so many compliments about it.

This Lasagana is just what you need for tonights family dinner. The wife and the kids will crave more of this mouthwatering dish!

Needed Items

  • Pasta
  • cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Pre-Heat Oven to 300
  3. Add Chop vegatbles to cook with Ground Beef
  4. Add Parsley to Garnish
